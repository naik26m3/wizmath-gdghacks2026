You

What does the slope of a line tell me?

Arcane

Slope is the line's rate of change — for every step right along x, how far it climbs or falls along y. A slope of 2 means the line rises 2 for every 1 it travels.

Try graphing y = 2x + 1 and watch the rise vs. run.

You

And the y-intercept?

Arcane

The y-intercept is where the line crosses the vertical axis — the value of y when x is zero. In y = mx + b, that's b.
